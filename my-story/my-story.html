My story

Everyone has those “crossroads moments” in life, when a new path must be chosen. After many years in science education, I arrived in that moment with a lot of experience: teaching, proofreading and copyediting, and fiber artistry, all while being a mother and grandmother, with all of the organizational skill set that requires. Web development was the obvious choice to use it all: markup and editing, design, language, and problem-solving.

I enjoy the detail and the creative problem-solving of coding. It is so satisfying to take a website idea and making it all work -- and look fabulous! I know that ease of collaboration, trouble-shooting, and updating are important to my clients, and I approach every project with this foundation of correct, concise, and elegant code in mind.
